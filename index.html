I am a professor of Algebraic Geometry at the KdV Institute for Mathematics of the University of Amsterdam. I enjoy thinking and talking about arithmetic geometry, homotopy theory, formal mathematics, and the prospects of automated proof.

See also my google scholar and github profiles.

The other staff members of the Algebraic Geometry group are Gerard van der Geer, Arno Kret, Diletta Martinelli, and Mingmin Shen.

We run a local Arithmetic and Algebraic Geometry seminar and take part in various national seminars, including the Belgian-Dutch Algebraic Geometry Days and the Intercity Number Theory Seminar.
