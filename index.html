I am a professor of Algebraic Geometry at the KdV Institute for Mathematics of the University of Amsterdam. I enjoy thinking and talking about a broad range of mathematical topics, and lately have been interested in many things related to arithmetic geometry, homotopy theory, or computer assisted proofs.

See also my google scholar and github profiles.

AG Group

The other staff members of the Algebraic Geometry group are Gerard van der Geer, Arno Kret, Diletta Martinelli, and Mingmin Shen.

We run a local Arithmetic and Algebraic Geometry seminar and take part in various national seminars, including the Belgian-Dutch Algebraic Geometry Days, the Intercity Number Theory Seminar, and the Intercity Geometry Seminar (archive).

My current and former PhD students and postdocs include: Dhyan Aranha, Simon Pepin Lehalleur, Zhouhang Mao, Noah Olander, Dion Leijnse, Wouter Rienks, Zoë Schroot, Maciek Zdanowicz, Wessel Bindt, Chris Lazda, Maxim Mornev, Lance Gurney, Christophe Debry.
